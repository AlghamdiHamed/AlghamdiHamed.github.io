# Biography

I am a PhD researcher in the School of Computing at Newcastle University. 
My research focuses on privacy-preserving delegated federated learning for resource-constrained IoT environments.

My current work investigates how cryptographic techniques, including homomorphic encryption, can support efficient and secure delegation in federated learning.
